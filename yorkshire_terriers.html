Phil Naylor’s Journey with Yorkshire Terriers

Phil Naylor bought his first Yorkshire Terrier in 1969 — a lovely female named Daisy May. Believing she had great potential as a mother, Phil bred her, and in 1971 welcomed his first Champion: Brave Warrior of Naylenor, affectionately known as Greg
There was no stopping Phil after that. By 1975, he had produced three more Champions:
  • Blue Monarch
  • Regal Warrior
  • Battle Cry
